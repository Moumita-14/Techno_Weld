Welcome to Techno Weld

TECHNOWELD started its journey as a NDT & PWHT service provider in the year of 1990 at DURGAPUR & was founded by Mr AMAL MUKHERJEE (PROPRIETOR).

In the year of 2016 Mechanical & Chemical Lab was set up and got accredited by NABL (ISO 17025) for Mechanical & Chemical Testing TC 5567. We value our client’s objectives & goals by providing accurate test results within due time & help them achieve their desired/required quality for the job.

The company has acquired the ISO: 17025:2017, quality systems in the year 2003. The company strives ahead to acquire latest technology to cope with the present day challenges in the field of NDT. The Company today is well established leader in the field of Most of the Non-Destructive Testing methods all over Indi. As on date the Company has dedicated work force of around 500 people under its wing in different categories.
