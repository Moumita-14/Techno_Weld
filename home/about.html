Welcome to the World of Techno Weld

Techno Weld was founded in 1990 and started its activities in a humble way in the field of Radiography, one of the Non Destructive Testing methods. The hard work, sincerity and dedication put together have brought the company to its present standing with the inclusion of all the NDT test methods under its wing. This includes test methods like Ultrasonic, Magnetic particle, Liquid Penetrant, Eddy current etc..

In the year of 2016 Mechanical & Chemical Lab was set up and got accredited by NABL (ISO 17025) for Mechanical & Chemical Testing TC 5567.

The Company today is well established leader in the field of Most of the Non-Destructive Testing methods all over India.
